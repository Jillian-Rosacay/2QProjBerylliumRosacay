STRAY KIDS' RELEASES

Discography

In their 5 years of being active, Stray Kids has released 8 extended plays (EPs), 2 studio albums, 3 compilation albums, 1 repackage album and 9 singles. Among them are the title tracks Hellevator, District 9, My Pace, I Am YOU, Miroh, Side Effects, Levanter, God's Menu, Back Door, and Thunderous.

You can access the Spotify page for Stray Kids' music through the icon above!


VLive

Stray Kids has a VLive channel! VLive is a streaming service where South Korean artists can broadcast live to their fans. Bang Chan hosts weekly VLives called "Chan's Room" where he talks to fans, occassionally featuring other members. Hyunjin hosts boradcasts where he practices dance and talks with viewers.

These are just a few examples of what they do on VLive. You can access Stray Kids' VLive channel through the VLive icon above!


YouTube

Stray Kids has multiple contents released on their youtube channel. From music videos to vlogs, they go all in in their channel! Here's a list of some of the series of videos they have there:

  1. SKZ-PLAYER - A collection of video projects by the members that were not formally released. These could include song and dance covers and special tracks.
  2. SKZ-RECORD - A collection of songs by the members that were not formally released.
  3. SKZ-TALKER- A series that shows what the members are doing during album promotions and their schedules.
  4. SKZ VLOG - A series that shows what members do on their day-offs and vacation.
  5. SKZ-CODE - A series where the members play games and have fun. Currently, Season 1 is over!
  6. 아리까리한 KITCHEN (Mysterious KITCHEN) - A cooking show featuring Lee Know, Han, and I.N where they make meals that the members want. Currently, Season 1 is over!
  7. Two Kids Room - A series where two members sit down and talk with each other. They have heart-to-hearts, recall memories together, and crack jokes!
  8. Two Kids Room +1 - A series where three members sit down and talk with each other. Basically Two Kids Room but with one more person!
  9. One Kid's Room - A series where after a member takes a MBTI test, he talks about his personality and the others talk about him too. Currently this series is over!
  10. ♥ Kids Room - A series where each member searches for their "soulmate" by asking questions to the others and picking the answer they like. Whoever is chosen at the end wins. Currently, this series is over!
  11. Two Kids Song - A series where members are randomly paired together and make a song. Currently, this series is over!
  12. [SKZ SONG CAMP] Howl in Harmony - A series where the members are divided into 3 sub-units to make 3 different songs for their album, Noeasy. Currently, this series is over!
  13. Album Specials - Whenever Stray Kids release a new album, they make content during the promotion of the album.

You can access Stray Kids' YouTube channel through the Youtube icon above!
