HWANG HYUNJIN

Hyunjin (현진 a.k.a Hwang Hyunjin) is the Korean visual of Stray Kids! His birthday is on March 20, 2000 (Pisces) and is currently 21 years old. He attended SOPA, School of Performing Arts, where he studied Practical Dance. He was a trainee for two years. He is part of the sub-unit Dance Racha. He is currently roomates with Lee Know and Seungmin.

Fun Facts!
