BANG CHAN

Bang Chan (방찬 a.k.a Christopher Bang) is the Korean-Australian leader of Stray Kids! His birthday is on October 3, 1997 (Libra) and is currently 24 years old. Bang Chan was a trainee for around 7 years, making him the member to undergo the longest training period. He formed a unit called 3RACHA with HAN and Changbin before the group's debut. He is currently roomates with Changbin and Felix.

Fun Facts!

- He has dimples when he smiles!

- His stage name as a member of 3RACHA is CB97.

- His MBTI is ENFJ-T.

LEE KNOW

Lee Know (리노 a.k.a Lee Minho) is the Korean Dancing Gem of Stray Kids! His birthday is on October 25, 1998 (Scorpio) and is currently 23 years old. Lee Know was actually eliminated in the survival show, but was eventually added back in the group by JYP. Before debuting with Stray Kids, he used to be a dancer for BTS! He is currently roommates with Hyunjin and Seungmin.

CHANGBIN

Changbin appeared in Show Me The Money 9, a Korean show where rappers compete against each other. He is currently roomates with Bang Chan and Felix.
