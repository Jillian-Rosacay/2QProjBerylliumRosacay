Who are Stray Kids?

Hey! You wanna come in?

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group that debuted under JYP Entertainment! They were formed under a survival show of the same name in 2017 and debuted officially on March 25, 2018. The group started out with 9 members but now, they only have 8 members.

The group has garnered more attention from both local and international fans after the release of their song, God's Menu.

In the rightmost picture, from left to right, top to bottom. Changbin, Felix, I.N., Lee Know, Han, Seungmin, Hyunjin, and Bang Chan.

Stray Kids call their fandom "STAY". It comes from "Stray" and they removed the letter R because STAY are the reason why Stray Kids exists. It is also an adorable homage to the group's tagline "You Make Stray Kids STAY". We love a group that loves their fans as much as their fans love them!
